Brownies Cake Recipe

Description

Bake these easy brownies whenever you need to quickly satisfy your sweet tooth. Made with kitchen staples, you'll come back to this easy brownie recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups - White Sugar
  • Flour
  • 250g - Melted Butter
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 cup - Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tbspn - Vanilla Extract
  • i tbspn - Baking Powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • Walnuts (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mix Dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix the Wet ingredients in a different bowl.
  3. Mix the two
  4. Preheat oven to 250 degrees Celcius
  5. Spread the butter into a pan
  6. Add the micture to the pan
  7. Bake in preheated Oven yntil top is dry
  8. Cool and Cut into squares or as preferred
