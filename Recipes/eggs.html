Fried Eggs Recipe

Description

Enjoy this Tasty Recipe for one as you begin your day

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbspn Cooking Oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat up pan and add the Cooking Oil.
  2. Break the 2 Eggs into a bowl and add the Salt and the Black Pepper.
  3. cook until the edges are brown and crispy and turn over the eggs.
  4. Serve while hot and Enjoy.
